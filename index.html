process

How DevAI works

Four phases, one seamless loop.

01

You describe the task

Plain English. No specs, no boilerplate. Just tell DevAI what you need.

02

Agent plans the approach

DevAI reasons about the problem, picks the right stack, and outlines the implementation steps.

03

Code is written & verified

The agent writes code, runs mental tests, catches edge cases, and refines until it's solid.

04

You get a full explanation

Every decision explained. You understand what was built and why — not just the output.
