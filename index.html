process

How KevinAI works

Four phases. One seamless loop.

01

Describe in plain English

No tickets, no specs. Just tell KevinAI what you need. Optionally attach files.

02

Agent reads and reasons

KevinAI reads your files, understands the full context, and builds a plan before writing a single line of code.

03

Code is written and verified

Step-by-step execution. If editing a file, you get the complete modified version back — not a diff, the whole thing.

04

Iterate in the same conversation

Follow up, refine, and build on previous answers. KevinAI remembers everything in the conversation.
